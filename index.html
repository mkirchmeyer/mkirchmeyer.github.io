Continuous PDE Dynamics Forecasting with Implicit Neural Representations
Y. Yin*, M. Kirchmeyer*, J-Y. Franceschi*, A. Rakotomamonjy, P. Gallinari (*equal contribution)
Under review
arXiv / code /

We propose DINO, a novel continuous-time continuous-space data-driven PDE forecasting model with extensive spatiotemporal extrapolation capabilities including generalization to unseen sparse meshes and resolutions. DINo combines a sequential neural ODE model with Implicit Neural Representations (INRs). Checkout our videos on our github page !

Mapping conditional distributions for domain adaptation under generalized target shift
M. Kirchmeyer, A. Rakotomamonjy, E. de B√©zenac, P. Gallinari
ICLR 2022. Also presented at CAp 2022 (oral)
OpenReview / code / slides / poster / video

We propose OSTAR a new deep learning method to align pretrained representations when both class-conditional and marginal distributions differ at test time. OSTAR has strong theoretical guarantees and introduces useful regularization biases in the NN thanks to Optimal Transport.

Unsupervised domain adaptation with non-stochastic missing data
M. Kirchmeyer, P. Gallinari, A. Rakotomamonjy, A. Mantrach
ECML-PKDD 2021 - Data Mining and Knowledge Discovery journal.
pdf / code / slides

We propose a new unsupervised domain adaptation method to impute non-stochastic missing data using supervision from another domain. Applications to cold-start problems in recommender systems and imputation problems in computer vision.
